School Leadership

Welcome to Abela

Abela supports children through strong academics, good manners, confidence-building routines, reading culture, and attentive care. We believe each child grows best when school expectations are clear, teaching is consistent, and families feel respected and included.

From pre-primary readiness to upper primary development, our focus is to help learners think clearly, speak confidently, and act with responsibility in school and at home.

Our direction

Academic seriousness with warm care

Abela combines purposeful classroom teaching, character formation, and daily routines that help children become confident learners and respectful members of the community.

  • • Reading and numeracy habits built early
  • • English confidence with Kiswahili support
  • • School-home partnership for each child
Learning journey

One pathway, carefully guided from pre-primary to upper primary.

01

Pre-Primary Foundation

Children build school readiness through early literacy sounds, number awareness, social skills, and guided routines that introduce discipline, curiosity, and confidence.

02

Lower Primary: Standard I–II

Learners strengthen reading fluency, writing habits, and core numeracy while using English daily and receiving supportive Kiswahili guidance where needed.

03

Upper Primary: Standard III–VI

Students deepen subject understanding, independent study habits, and responsible conduct with clear Tanzania curriculum alignment and steady teacher mentorship.
