# Abela Pre & Primary School Photoshoot Shot List

## Must-have production shots

1. Homepage hero: smiling pupils in clean uniform, bright classroom or campus entrance.
2. Teacher support: teacher bending beside a pupil writing in an exercise book.
3. Pre-primary: play-based learning, blocks, colours, story time.
4. Primary: mathematics, English reading, science activity.
5. Admissions: parent and child visiting office or school gate.
6. School life: football, clubs, assembly, reading corner, meals.
7. Safety/trust: clean compound, supervised play, handwashing area.
8. Staff portraits: head teacher, administrator, class teachers.
9. Facilities: classroom, library corner, playground, transport if available.
10. Brand detail: school badge, uniforms, books, noticeboard, Tanzanian/Pwani environment.

## Style direction

Premium Tanzanian English-medium school, natural sunlight, clean uniforms, authentic Mkuranga/Pwani setting, no over-edited AI look, warm and trustworthy parent-facing emotion.
