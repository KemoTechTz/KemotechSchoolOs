Admission journey

Simple, clear and parent-friendly.

Our admissions process is designed to be clear, structured and parent-friendly.

01

Apply Online

Create a parent account and submit learner details through the application form.

02

School Visit

Tour the school, meet admissions staff and understand expectations.

03

Readiness Check

Age-appropriate interaction or assessment for correct class placement.

04

Confirmation

Receive admission status, fee guidance and joining instructions.

🧾

Required Documents

Birth certificate copy, passport photo, parent/guardian contacts, previous school report where applicable.

🕘

Time of Admission

Admissions are open during normal school office hours. Priority is given to early applications before class spaces are filled.

🏫

Visit the School

Families are encouraged to book a visit and experience the classrooms, routines and learning environment.
